    Question

    The average income of a large sample of students who all work at a law firm is 500 euros (per month), with a standard deviation of 100 euros. The average income is normally distributed. What is the proportion of students with an income higher than 450 euros per month?


    1. FALSE: 0.50
    2. FALSE: 0.56
    3. TRUE: 0.69
    4. FALSE: 0.31
    5. FALSE: 0.85

    Solution

    z = (450-500)/100 = 0.500 (it is half a sd from the mean). The Z Score Table shows for z = 0.50 the figure of 0.3085. This is the probability of an income below 450. Thus, the probability of an income higher than 450 euros per month is 1-0.3085= 0.6915.
