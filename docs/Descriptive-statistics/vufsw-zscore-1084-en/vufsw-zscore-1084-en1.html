    Suppose one of the visitors has a physical hygiene score of 0.88 on day three. What is his / her standardized score (z-score)?


    1. TRUE: -0.14
    2. FALSE: -0.28
    3. FALSE: -3.38
    4. FALSE: -4.01
    5. FALSE: -1.24

    Solution

    The formula is: z = (observation - mean) / standard deviation

    Thus, z = (0.88 - 0.98) / 0.71 = -0.14
