    What happens to the correlation between two variables X and Y if we standardize them (in other words: if we transform them to their z-scores)?

    The correlation coefficient …


    1. TRUE: remains exactly the same.
    2. FALSE: becomes 1.00.
    3. FALSE: becomes 0.00.
    4. FALSE: can become either lower or higher depending on whether the association between X and Y is negative or positive.
    5. FALSE: can become either lower or higher depending on whether the difference between the means of X and Y is smaller or larger than 0.00.
