    In Boelen and van den Hout’s article, The Role of Threatening Misinterpretations and Avoidance in Emotional Problems After Loss (2008), they write the following sentence (from Statistical analyses): ‘As recommended by Aiken and West, in all regression analyses, predictors were centred to reduce ….’.     1. FALSE: Heteroscedasticity.

    3. TRUE: Homoscedasticity.
