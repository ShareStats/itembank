    Question

    The correlations between the items of two measurement instruments are as follows:

    From which measurement instrument will the internal consistency of the scale (the Cronbach’s alpha) be higher?


    1. TRUE: The Cronbach’s alpha from measurement instrument 1 is higher.
    2. FALSE: The Cronbach’s alpha from measurement instrument 2 is higher.
    3. FALSE: The Cronbach’s alpha’s of both measurement instruments are equally high.
    4. FALSE: Based on this information it is not possible to determine which Cronbach’s alpha is higher.

    Solution

    The Cronbach’s alpha from instrument 1 is higher. Because the items are higher correlated.
