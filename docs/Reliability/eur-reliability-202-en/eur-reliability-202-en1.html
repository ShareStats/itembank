    Question

    Consider the following statements about regression to the mean:

    1. Regression to the mean happens when the mean of a sub sample is not equal to the mean of the complete sample.

    2. The higher the reliability of a tests, the larger the effect of regression to the mean


    1. FALSE: Both statements are correct.
    2. TRUE: Statement 1 is correct and statement 2 is incorrect.
    3. FALSE: Statement 2 is correct and statement 1 is incorrect.
    4. FALSE: Both statements are incorrect.

    Solution

    Statement 1 is true: every sub sample that has a mean that differs from the sample mean will have a sub sample mean that is closer to the sample mean on a second occasion. Statement 2 is not true: the higher the reliability, the smaller the effect of regression to the mean.


    1. False
    2. True
    3. False
    4. False
