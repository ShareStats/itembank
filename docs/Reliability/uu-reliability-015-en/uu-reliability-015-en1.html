    Question

    The Media & Entertainment Management (MEM) program at Inholland University of Applied Sciences was rated very weak by the Education Inspectorate in 2011. In response to this reporting, research is being conducted into student satisfaction with this program. An experimental test version consists of 20 equivalent items. The reliability rxx’ of this test is .85. For the real study, the researcher must shorten the test by removing five items.

    The estimated reliability of the scores of the shortened test (r ̂VVV’) is then:


    1. FALSE: Lower than .65
    2. FALSE: Between .65 and .75
    3. TRUE: Between .75 and .85
    4. FALSE: Higher than .85

    Solution

    Explanation: The formula for the extension factor is v - k′k\frac {k'}{k} where k’is the number of questions on the new test (here 20-5=15 questions) and k is the number of questions on the original test (here:20). Filling in gives v= 1520=0.75\frac {15}{20}= 0.75

    Thus, the correct answer is Option C.
