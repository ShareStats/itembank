    Below you find the output of a factor analyses on 8 items measuring extraversion.

    Table 1
    Factor Eigenvalue
    1 2.2

    Calculate the eigenvalue of the 6th Factor.


    Solution

    The eigenvalue of the 6th factor can be calculated as follows. The sum of the eigenvalues of 8 variables equals 8. The sum of the eigenvalues of the factors present in the table is: 2.2+1.8+1.1+.9+.8+.3+.3=7.42.2+1.8+1.1+.9+.8+.3+.3=7.4. Thus, the eigenvalue of the 6th factor is: 8‐7.4=.68‐7.4= .6.
