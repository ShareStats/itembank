    Question

    A teacher is interested in the association (the relationship) between the number of hours that his students have studied (x) and their examination marks (y). He performs a regression analysis with these two variables. The slope of the regression line can be interpreted as …


    1. FALSE: The grade that a student receives when he or she has studied for one hour.
    2. FALSE: The average grade of all students in this sample.
    3. TRUE: The average increase / decrease of the grade if a student studies one extra hour.
    4. FALSE: None of the above

    Solution

    it is only the slope, without the intercept. Therefore it is not true. If it was true it was the slope plus the intercept. The right answer is: the average increase / decrease of the grade if a student studies one extra hour.
