    Question

    Given is the following quadratic sum associated with a repeated measures ANOVA: SSpersons=k*Σ(Mpi−Mgrand)2SS_{persons} = k * Σ(M_{pi} - M_{grand})^2. What does the formula say in the parentheses (Mpi−Mgrand)(M_{pi} - M_{grand})?


    1. TRUE: The deviation of the mean of person i from the overall mean.
    2. FALSE: The deviation of the mean of time i from the overall mean.
    3. FALSE: The deviation of the average person from the overall mean.
    4. FALSE: The deviation of the mean time from the overall mean.
