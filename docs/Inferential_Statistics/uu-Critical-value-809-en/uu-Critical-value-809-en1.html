    Question

    The scores in a sample of n = 12 have mean M = 5 and standard error sM = 1. A two-sided t-test with Î±\alpha = 5% results in a test statistic of t = 2.5. What are the lower and upper bounds of the 95% confidence interval, respectively?


    1. FALSE: The confidence interval is irrelevant because the t-test is not significant.
    2. FALSE: Lower limit = 2.5, upper limit = 7.5.
    3. FALSE: Lower limit = 2.821, upper limit = 7.179.
    4. TRUE: Lower limit = 2.799, upper limit = 7.201.
