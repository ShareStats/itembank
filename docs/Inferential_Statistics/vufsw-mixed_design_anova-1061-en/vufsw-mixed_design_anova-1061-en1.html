    Question

    Mixed design ANOVAs:


    1. FALSE: Only include a mixture of within-group and repeated-measures variables.
    2. FALSE: Require at least four independent variables.
    3. TRUE: None of the other answers is correct
    4. FALSE: Cannot combine repeated-measures and independent designs.

    Solution

    Mixed design ANOVAs contain a mixture of between- groups and repeated measure variables, require at least two independent variables and can combine repeated-measures and independent designs. Therefore none of the answers is correct.
