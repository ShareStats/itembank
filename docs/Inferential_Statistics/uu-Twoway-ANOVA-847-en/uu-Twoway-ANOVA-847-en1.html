    Question

    Researchers want to know whether men and women differ in their appreciation of a particular advertising campaign (measured on a scale of 0-10). In addition, the researchers are also interested in the effect of educational level (measured with: mbo or lower, hbo, wo), which is also included in the analysis.

    Which analysis technique is most suitable for this research?


    1. FALSE: One-way ANOVA
    2. FALSE: One-way ANCOVA
    3. TRUE: Two-way ANOVA
    4. FALSE: Two-way ANCOVA

    Solution

    Dependent variable: appreciation of a particular advertising campaign (interval level of measurement)

    First independent variable: sex (difference between men and women; measurement level: nominal)

    Second independent variable: educational level (measurement level: nominal/ordinal)

    Both independent variables are categorical, so the most suitable technique here is a Two-way ANOVA.
