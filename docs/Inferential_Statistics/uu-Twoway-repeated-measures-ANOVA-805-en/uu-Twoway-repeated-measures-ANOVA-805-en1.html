Male teachers are shown four photographs to give their first impression of that child. The randomly presented photos are of an attractive boy, an unattractive boy, an attractive girl and an unattractive girl. The teachers assign a value for the child’s friendliness and they estimate intelligence.

    How would you describe the design of this study?
