    For comparing two averages, a confidence interval for a difference of two averages μ1 - μ2 is calculated. The confidence interval contains only negative numbers. This means that


    1. TRUE: Μ1 is smaller than μ2
    2. FALSE: Μ1 is greater than μ2
    3. FALSE: Both μ1 and μ2 cannot have been positive

    1. Μ1 is smaller than μ2: Correct
    2. Μ1 is greater than μ2: Incorrect
    3. Both μ1 and μ2 cannot have been positive: Incorrect
