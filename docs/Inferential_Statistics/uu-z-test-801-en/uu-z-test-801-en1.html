    Question

    The number of violent crimes in the Netherlands is normally distributed with an average μ=2.6\mu = 2.6 per day and a standard deviation σ=0.5\sigma = 0.5. One researcher hypothesized that violent crime rates increase in hot weather. In a sample of 25 warm days, an average of 2.8 violent crimes per day is found. The researcher performs a one-sided z-test. At what significance level α\alpha Is the null hypothesis rejected?


    1. TRUE: The H0H_0 is rejected at α\alpha = 5%, but not at α\alpha = 1%.
    2. FALSE: The H0H_0 is rejected at α\alpha = 1%, but not at α\alpha = 5%.
    3. FALSE: The H0H_0 becomes both at α\alpha = 5% as at α\alpha = 1% rejected.
    4. FALSE: The H0H_0 is rejected neither at α\alpha = 5% nor at α\alpha = 1% rejected.
