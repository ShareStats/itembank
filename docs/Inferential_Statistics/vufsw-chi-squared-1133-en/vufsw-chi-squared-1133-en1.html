    Question

    An investigation among the members of a large trade union organization on the relationship between their degree of involvement (low, middle, high) at the trade union and their participation in a strike (yes, no) provides the following data:


    		 involvement

    		low middle  high                  total
    participation in strike



    yes   30                            25   70                  125
    no 70 25   30                  125
    total 100 50                      100                  250

    What is the critical chi square value in this test with a significance level  alpha= 0.05?


    1. FALSE: 1.96
    2. FALSE: 3.84
    3. TRUE: 5.99
    4. FALSE: 124.3

    Solution

    The degrees of freedom (df) is 2 and alpha=0.05, so the critical value of chi square is 5.99 (see table)
    (1.96 is the critical value of the t distribution with more than 100 degrees of freedom. )
    (3.84 is the critical value of the chi square distribution if there is only one degree of freedom)
    (124.3 is the critical value of the chi square distribution if there are 100 degrees of freedom)


    1. False
    2. False
    3. True
    4. False
