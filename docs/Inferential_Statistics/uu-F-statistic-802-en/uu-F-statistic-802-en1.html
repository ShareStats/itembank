    Question

    A sample of 235 students in the Faculty of Social Sciences at Utrecht University are given a questionnaire to complete about feelings. One of the questions read, “What do you think about your own height?” The response categories were: too short, about right, too tall. A total score was calculated for all students; it measures students’ self-esteem. The results of the self-esteem scores are compared between the three height-sensitive groups and between boys and girls. The F-ratio for the interaction effect between height-feelings and gender is 4.984.98 with a pp-value of .008.008.

    How is the pp-value of .008.008 calculated?


    1. TRUE: The area to the right of F=4.98F = 4.98 Under the F-distribution.
    2. FALSE: The area to the left of F=4.98F = 4.98 Under the F-distribution.
    3. FALSE: The area to the left of F=−4.98F = −4.98 en rechts van F=4.98F = 4.98 Under the F-distribution.
    4. FALSE: The area between F=−4.98F = -4.98 and F=4.98F = 4.98 under the F distribution.
