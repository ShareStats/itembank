    Question

    In a study of influences on healing after major surgery, the following variables are involved: disease insight and IQ. In a mediator analysis examining whether the influence of IQ is through disease insight, the researcher finds the following relationships (expressed as regression coefficients):

    1. b = .64 of IQ -> healing. (2) b = .56 from IQ -> disease insight. (3) b = .55 of disease insight -> healing (where IQ is also included as a predictor in the model)

    The final analysis in this mediator analysis is a regression analysis with IQ and disease insight as predictors and healing as dependent variableâ€¦.


    1. TRUE: Where you expect regression coefficient (1) to decrease.
    2. FALSE: Where you expect regression coefficient (2) to decrease.
    3. FALSE: Where you expect regression coefficient (3) to decrease.
    4. FALSE: Where you expect all three regression coefficients to decrease.

    Solution

    To test mediation, we examine the four steps (see question 12). The final step is to see if the relationship between IQ and healing is still significant when controlling for disease insight?

    When there is mediation, the relationship between IQ and healing will be weaker when disease insight is also included in the analysis. So you would expect regression coefficient (1) to decrease.
