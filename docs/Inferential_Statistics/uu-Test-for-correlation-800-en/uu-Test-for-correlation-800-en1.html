    Question

    A researcher administered questionnaires to 25 randomly selected employees of a company. The questionnaires measure feelings of worry, stress level, and other social-emotional aspects, among others. The researcher wants to test whether the correlation between worry and stress is significant among α=.01\alpha=.01 and wants a tt-test. TRUE is the minimum value of the correlation it must find so that the tt-test will yield significant results?


    1. TRUE: .505
    2. FALSE: .487
