    Question

    A psychologist is interested in the effect of various therapies on stress. A large group of participants is randomly divided into four groups. The first three groups each are assigned to different therapies. The fourth group is used as a control group. Stress is measured on a scale from 0-35. The mean stress scores for the four groups are to be compared at the end of the study. What do we call this research design?


    1. FALSE: Within subjects design
    2. TRUE: Between subjects design
    3. FALSE: Mixed design (both between and within)
    4. FALSE: Repeated measures design
