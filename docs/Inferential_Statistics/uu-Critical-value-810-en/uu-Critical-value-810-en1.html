    Question

    In a science quiz, 10 Utrecht and 10 Amsterdam students compete against each other. The Utrecht students score 7 points more than the Amsterdam students, and the pooled variance of the scores equals 45. Do the scores of the Utrecht and Amsterdam students differ significantly from each other in a 2-sided test with α\alpha = 5%?


    1. FALSE: No, because the test statistic is 1.04 and the critical limits are ± 2.101.
    2. FALSE: No, because the test statistic is 1.04 and the critical limits are ± 2.262.
    3. TRUE: Yes, because the test statistic is 2.33 and the critical limits are ± 2.101.
