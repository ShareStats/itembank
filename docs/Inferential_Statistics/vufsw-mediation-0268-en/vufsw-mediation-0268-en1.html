    Question

    In which of the following research designs is there a possible mediation effect?


    1. TRUE: A study that studies whether the effect of parental educational attainment on the child’s income disappears when educational attainment is taken into account
    2. FALSE: A study examining whether the effect of parental educational attainment on the child’s income disappears when grandparents’ socioeconomic status is taken into account
    3. FALSE: A study examining whether the effect of parental education level on the child’s income differs between boys and girls
    4. FALSE: A study examining whether both parental education level and grandparents’ socioeconomic status have an effect on the child’s income

    Solution

    Here you have a design TRUE to test 2 separate effects on the dependent Y (child’s income): the effect of the independent X1 (parents’ education level) and the independent X2 (grandparents’ socioeconomic status).


    1. True
    2. False
    3. False
    4. False
