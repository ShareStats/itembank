    Question

    In an ANCOVA model with interaction, the following regression equation is estimated, where x1x_{1} is the covariate and z is the Factor with two levels (dummy variable): ŷ=10+2x1+3z−4x1z\hat{y} = 10+2x_{1}+3z-4x_{1}z.

    Calculate the predicted y score for a score 5 on the covariate and a score 1 on the dummy variable.


    1. TRUE: 3
    2. FALSE: 17
    3. FALSE: 19
    4. FALSE: 43

    Solution

    Enter x1=5x_{1} = 5 and z=1z = 1 gives:

    Predicted y=10+2×5+3×1−4×5×1=10+10+3−20=3y = 10+2 \times 5+3 \times 1-4 \times 5 \times 1 = 10+10+3-20 = 3.
