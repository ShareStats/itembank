    Question

    What is the best description of a sample distribution?


    1. FALSE: T is the distribution of variation within a given sample (sample variability), such as the standard deviations of the sample.
    2. FALSE: It is the distribution of units (units) from which we draw a sample, such as a random group of respondents.
    3. TRUE: It is the distribution of a sample statistic (sample statistic), such as sample proportion or sample mean.
    4. FALSE: It is the distribution of population parameters (population parameters), such as population proportions or population averages.
