    Question

    The linear relationship between the degree of experience of pain (Y) during an anaesthetic injection and the degree of dentition anxiety (X1) and age (X2) in a regression formula is equal to  Y  = 1.00 + 0.23 X1 + 0.02 X2. A person of 20 years old is pretty anxious about the dentist. He/she has an anxiety score of 24. What is the predicted value for the pain of this person?


    1. FALSE: 0.23
    2. TRUE: 6.92
    3. FALSE: 5.52
    4. FALSE: 1.00

    Solution

    It is 6,92 (1,00+ 0,23*24+0,02*20)
