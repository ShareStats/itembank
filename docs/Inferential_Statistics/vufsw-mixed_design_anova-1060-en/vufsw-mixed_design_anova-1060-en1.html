    Question

    Which of the following is an example of a mixed design?


    1. FALSE: An investigation of the effect that an employee’s gender has on the age of attaining a senior management position
    2. TRUE: None of the other answers describes a mixed design
    3. FALSE: An investigation of the effect that the gender of an accountant has on month’s-end reporting errors among accountants that followed training and accountants that did not follow training
    4. FALSE: An investigation of the effect that gender of employees has on choice of training programme adopted.

    Solution

    There are no repeated measurement in this design.
    (An employee can either follow or not follow the training not both. In this case, there is a 2-way ANOVA but not a repeated measures design.)
