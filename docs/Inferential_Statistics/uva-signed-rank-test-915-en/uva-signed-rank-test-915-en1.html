The scores are ranked (1 through 6). The difference between group A and group B is: A - B = 1. A possible ranking of the participants in the two groups is


    1. FALSE: Order of rank of group A is (1, 2, 3) and of group B (4, 5, 6)
    2. FALSE: Order of rank of group A is (4, 5, 6) and of group B (1, 2, 3)
    3. TRUE: Ranking order of group A is (1, 5, 6) and of group B (2, 3, 4)

    1. Rank order of group A is (1, 2, 3) and of group B is (4, 5, 6): Incorrect
    2. Rank of group A is (4, 5, 6) and of group B (1, 2, 3): Incorrect
    3. Rank of group A is (1, 5, 6) and of group B (2, 3, 4): Correct
