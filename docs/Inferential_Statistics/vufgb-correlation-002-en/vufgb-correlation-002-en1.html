    Question

    In a sample, a correlation of 0.64 is found between two variables x and y. The standard deviation of x is 24, and the standard deviation of y is 18.

    Calculate the regression coefficient b.


    1. TRUE: 0.48
    2. FALSE: 0.55
    3. FALSE: 0.64
    4. FALSE: 0.85

    Solution

    Use the formula on the formula sheet for correlation (Chapter 9).

    Filling it in gives 0.64=2418×b0.64 = \frac{24}{18 \times b}. Describe gives b=0.64×1824=0.48b = 0.64 \times \frac{18}{24} = 0.48.


    1. Correct
    2. Incorrect
    3. Incorrect
    4. Incorrect
