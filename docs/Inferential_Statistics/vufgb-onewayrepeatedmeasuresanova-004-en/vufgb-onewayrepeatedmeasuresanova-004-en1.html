    Question

    An experiment is designed to investigate the effect of cognitive behavioral therapy on anxiety symptoms specifically in children diagnosed with autism. 100 children diagnosed with autism are randomly assigned to either the experimental group or control group. Over a three-month period, anxiety symptoms are then measured bi-weekly. Finally, a repeated measures ANOVA model is established. What between-subject and within-subject factors are in the model?


    1. FALSE: Etween-subject: Autism; Within-subject: Time.
    2. FALSE: Between-subject: Time; Within-subject: Autism.
    3. TRUE: Between-subject: Group; Within-subject: Time.
    4. FALSE: Between-subject: Time; Within-subject: Group.
