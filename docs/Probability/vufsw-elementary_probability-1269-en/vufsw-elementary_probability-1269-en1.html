    Question

    The magazine ‘Cosmopolitan’ suggests that women who skip breakfast have a greater chance of being pregnant with a girl, while women who do have breakfast are more likely to be pregnant with a boy. To test this, we took a sample of pregnant women who were already familiar with the sex of their child. We asked them about the sex of their baby and whether or not they had breakfast every day. Given that a woman does have breakfast, what is the likelihood of a boy?


    1. FALSE: The number that gets a boy and not had breakfast / the number that gets a boy and had breakfast.
    2. TRUE: The number that gets a boy and had breakfast / the number that had breakfast.
    3. FALSE: The number that gets a boy and had breakfast / the number that gets a boy receives and not had breakfast.
    4. FALSE: The number that gets a boy and had breakfast / the number that not had breakfast.

    Solution

    The likelihood of getting a boy for a woman that did have breakfast is: The number that gets a boy and had breakfast / the number that had breakfast.


    1. False
    2. True
    3. False
    4. False
