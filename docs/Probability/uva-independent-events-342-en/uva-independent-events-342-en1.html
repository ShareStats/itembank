    Given the following two assertions about these data:______________. 1: Left-handedness and right-handedness are independent, because P(right-handed ∩ left-handed) = 0. 2: Being artistically well-developed and left-handedness are independent because P(left-handed ∩ artistically well developed) = P(artistically well developed). Which of these statements is correct?


    1. FALSE: Assertions 1 and 2 are both correct.
    2. FALSE: Assertion 1 is true, assertion 2 is false.
    3. FALSE: Assertion 1 is false, assertion 2 is true.

    1. Assertions 1 and 2 are both correct: Incorrect
