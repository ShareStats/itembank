    Question

    Which of the following variables is not a continuous variable?


    1. FALSE: The time required to solve a sum.
    2. FALSE: Temperature.
    3. FALSE: Weight.
    4. TRUE: Number of children per family.

    1. Time is a continuous variable. 3.5 minutes or 6.93 seconds are possible values.
    2. Temperature is a continuous variable. It could be 13.5 degrees outside or the oven could be 181.7 degrees hot.
    3. Weight is a continuous variable. A person could weigh 71.7 pounds or you could have 145.6 grams of sugar left in the bag.
    4. This answer is correct. We are counting the number of children. This is a discrete variable.
