    Question

    Suppose a teacher wants to test whether the average number of days that students spend a week on statistics is higher than 3 days.
    Which type of test would he best use?


    1. TRUE: One-sample t-test
    2. FALSE: Independent t-test
    3. FALSE: Chi-square test
    4. FALSE: Simple regression

    Solution

    It is a one sample t test, because The One Sample t Test** is commonly used to test the following:**

    1. Statistical difference between a sample mean and a known or hypothesized value of the mean in the population.
    2. Statistical difference between the sample mean and the sample midpoint of the test variable.

    The independent t-test, is an inferential statistical test that determines whether there is a statistically significant difference between the means in two unrelated groups.

     Use the chi-square test for independence to determine whether there is a significant relationship between two categorical variables.

    Use a simple regression to describe the relationships between an independent variable and a dependent variable.
