    Question

    The table below shows the data of 9 students concerning their exam grade (Y), measured on a scale from 0 to 100, and absence from lecture (X), measured in the number of missed lectures. The formula that belongs to the regression line is Y= 96.14+ 2.75*X. What is the estimated exam grade of a student who has not missed any lectures and therefore scores 0 on absence?

    X
    		0 3 6 4 9 2 15 8 5
    Y
    		98 86 80 82 71 92 55 76 82

    1. FALSE: 0
    2. FALSE: 2,75
    3. TRUE: 96,14
    4. FALSE: 96

    Solution

    The formula: Y= 96.14+ 2.75*X : if X is 0 than Y=96.14


    1. False
    2. False
    3. True
    4. False
