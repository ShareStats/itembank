We look at the corresponding estimated ANOVA model, but present it as a regression model with dummy variables. The formula is: ŷ=10+2Z1−2Z2\hat{y} = 10 + 2Z_1 - 2Z_2. What would the estimated model look like if group 1 were chosen as the reference category?
