    Question

    Given is a t-distribution with 23 degrees of freedom.

    A which t-value the 97.5% of the values is at the right side?


    1. FALSE: -2.500
    2. TRUE: -2.069
    3. FALSE: 2.069
    4. FALSE: 2.500

    Solution

    Look in the t-table at df=23df = 23.

    A t-value of 2.069 gives a right exceedance probability of 2.5%. This means 2.5% of the values lies at the right side. For a t-value of -2.069, 2.5% of the values lies at the left side, and so 97.5% of the values at the right side.

    Advice: Draw the t-distribution and put the t-values and the corresponding exceedance probability of the 4 answer options in the drawing!


    1. Incorrect
    2. Correct
    3. Incorrect
    4. Incorrect
