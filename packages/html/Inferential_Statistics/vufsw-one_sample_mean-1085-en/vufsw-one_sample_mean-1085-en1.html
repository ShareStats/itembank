    Question

    According to a survey of 55 men and 68 women of LINDA Magazine (February 2016), women cry 30 to 64 times a year, while men only cry between 6 and 17 times a year. Suppose the population mean “number of times to cry” for women is 45, with a standard deviation of 8. What is the z-score of a woman who cries 15 times a year?


    1. FALSE: -1.88
    2. TRUE: -3.75
    3. FALSE: -7.00
    4. FALSE: -30.92
    5. FALSE: -6.63

    Solution

    To calculate the z-score, you will find the difference between a value in the sample and the mean, and divide it by the standard deviation. Thus
    (15 - 45) / 8 = -3.75
