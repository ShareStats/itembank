    Question

    In a mixed design study, all subjects participate in [–I–] level(s) of the within-subjects variable, and [–II–] level(s) of the between-subjects variable.


    1. TRUE: I. all II. one
    2. FALSE: I. one II. all
    3. FALSE: I. all II. all
    4. FALSE: I. one II. one

    Solution

    Between group factor means that the participants are split in the categories of this factor. Within factor means that the all participants are tested in all conditions of this factor.
