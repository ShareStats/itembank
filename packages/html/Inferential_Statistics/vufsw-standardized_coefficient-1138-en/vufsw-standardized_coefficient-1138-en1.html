    Question

    A teacher is interested in the association between the number of hours that his students have studied (X) and their examination marks (Y). He performs a regression analysis with these two variables. The standardized regression coefficient (“beta” in SPSS) of the number of study hours can be interpreted as …


    1. TRUE: The number of standard deviations that the number increases/decreases when the number of study hours increases by one standard deviation.
    2. FALSE: The standard deviation of the number of study hours.
    3. FALSE: The average increase/decrease of the grade if the student studies one extra hour.
    4. FALSE: None of the above.

    Solution

    The correct answer is: the number of standard deviations that the number increases / decreases when the number of study hours increases by one standard deviation.
