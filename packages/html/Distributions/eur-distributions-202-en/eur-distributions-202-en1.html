    Question

    Evaluate the following statements about transformation of scores:

    1. After standardizing raw scores, the scores will be approximately normally distributed.

    2. When raw scores are normalized, a non-linear transformation is performed.


    1. FALSE: Statement 1 and statement 2 are both true
    2. FALSE: Statement 1 is true and statement 2 is false
    3. TRUE: Statement 1 is false and statement 2 is true
    4. FALSE: Statement 1 and statement 2 are both false

    Solution

    Statement 1 is false: after standardization, the scores have the same distribution as the raw scores, which is not necessarily normal.

    Statement 2 is correct: when scores are normalized, you have to perform a non-linear transformation in order to change the distribution of the raw scores (make them more normally distributed).


    1. False
    2. False
    3. True
    4. False
