    The individual weight of 500 males is normally distributed with a mean of 78 kg and a standard deviation of 6 kg. What is the percentage of males with a weight between 72 and 88 kg?


    1. TRUE: 79.4%
    2. FALSE: 68.2%
    3. FALSE: 99.7%
    4. FALSE: 88.3%
    5. FALSE: 60.1%

    Solution

    The empirical rule is a statistical rule which states that for a normal distribution, almost all data will fall within three standard deviations of the mean.
