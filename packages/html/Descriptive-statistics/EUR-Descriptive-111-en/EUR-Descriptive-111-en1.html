    Question

    On the exam of course 2.8, 189 students gave a correct response to the question “What is the population mean of drawing a head of a coin?” 71 students gave an incorrect answer.

    What is the standard deviation of the mean score of a head?


    Solution

    The standard deviation of drawing a head is .5×.5=.5\sqrt{.5 \times .5} = .5 (taking the square root of the variance).
